About us

Brick Connexion provides Fabrication and Maintenance services to the Construction, Electromechanical industries. We utilize qualified professionals, technicians and tradesmen to provide such services as consulting, estimating, drafting, project management, fabrication, quality control, commissioning, maintenance.

Brick Connexion works with clients to develop cost effective processes and procedures to meet the multiple demands of the projects. Our construction management team works with leading edge software applications, workface planning, scheduling, and problem processes.
